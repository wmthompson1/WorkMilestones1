Important Information

The Transition System Framework (TSF) is a platform developed and maintained by the Center for Change in Transition Services. You must be employed by a school, district, or ESD in the state of Washington to receive access to the TSF. For more information please visit the Center for Change in Transition Services website.
If you require access to the TSF, please email ccts@seattleu.edu with the followingn information, or have your district administrator provide acccess:
  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • E-Mail address
  • School(s) you will need access to
  • Which components of the TSF you will need access to:
    • Transition IEP Review (Indicator 13)
    • Post-School Survey (Indicator 14)
    • QuIST
