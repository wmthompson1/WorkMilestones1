Indicator 14 Categories

Youth who are no longer in secondary school, had IEPs in effect at the time they left school, and, within one year of leaving high school:

14-1. Higher Education

...had been enrolled full- or part-time in a community college (2 year) program or college/university (4 year) program for at least one complete term.

14-2. Competitive Employment

...had worked for pay at or above minimum wage in a setting with others who are nondisabled, for twenty (20) hours or more a week, for at least ninety (90) days. This includes military employment.

14-3. Some Other Postsecondary Education or Training

...had been enrolled on a full- or part-time basis in an education or training program (e.g., Job Corps, adult education, workforce development program, or vocational technical school which is less than a 2-year program) for at least one complete term.

14-4. Some Other Employment

...had worked for pay or have been self-employed in a setting with others who are nondisabled for a period of at least 90 days at any time in the first year since leaving high school. This includes working in a family business (e.g., farm, store, fishing, ranching, catering services, etc.).

Other / Not Engaged

...could not be categorized under 1, 2, 3, or 4.
These categories sum to the total number of respondents.
